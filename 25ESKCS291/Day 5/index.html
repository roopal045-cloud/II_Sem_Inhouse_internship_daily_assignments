About the Event

What is SKIT Hackathon?

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to participate?

Any undergraduate or graduate student enrolled in any higher education institute/college situated in Jaipur can sign up.

What if I don't have a structured idea yet?

Don't sweat it. The theme is fully Open Innovation. You can modify, refine, or pivot your project concept right up until the official hacking clock fires up.

What do I need to bring to campus?

Your setup rigs (laptops, component hardware, extension nodes), personal IDs, and standard overnight gear. Food and high-speed networks are entirely on us.

Secure Your Slot

Slots are highly constrained by hardware lab spacing thresholds. Ensure accuracy before processing submission frames.
